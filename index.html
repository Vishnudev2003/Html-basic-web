Food delivery refers to the process of ordering food from a restaurant or food provider and having it delivered to a customer's desired location. Food delivery can be either done by the restaurant's own staff or through a third-party delivery service.

With the advent of technology and online ordering platforms, food delivery has become increasingly popular and convenient for people who are looking to enjoy a meal from their favorite restaurant without leaving their home or office. Customers can browse menus, place orders, and make payments online, making the process quick and easy.

Food delivery has become an essential part of the food industry, particularly during the COVID-19 pandemic, where people have been encouraged to stay at home and practice social distancing. Many restaurants have adapted to this trend and have begun offering delivery services, making it easier for customers to enjoy their favorite meals while staying safe and healthy.
